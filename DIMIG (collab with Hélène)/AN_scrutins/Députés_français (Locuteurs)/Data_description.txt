------------------------------------------
Info on deputies's terms ('mandats' in French) 
and political bodies ('organes' in French) 
since the 11th legislature (June 1997). 
------------------------------------------

Source: https://data.assemblee-nationale.fr/acteurs/historique-des-deputes


---Sub-folder "0_raw"---

This sub-folder contains all files extracted from the National Assembly website on deputies and their political groups. 


---Sub-folder "1_extract_python"---

The extract_data.ipynb python code extracts the .json files and creates csv files ('mandats.csv' and 'organes.csv') for MPs and the political bodies they belong to.


---Sub-folder "2_cleaning_stata"---

The do-file "build_dataset.do" uses the csv files from the previous sub-folders to create two .dta datasets :

In the "mandats.dta" dataset, you can find all personal characteristics of the representatives, with their name, their birth place and time, and their occupation.
You can also find the type of term ("mandat") they occupy, the dates of this term and where they were elected. 

In the "organes.dta" dataset, you can find information about political bodies (the type of political body, the name and its description). 

With the identifier, we can merge the 2 datasets and obtain information about which political body each representative belongs to.
We cover the period February 1980 to July 2025. 


---Sub-folder "3_cleaning_stata"---

Contains the final dataset "mandats" (DTA and CSV formats). 

Note: this dataset includes information on political bodies from "organes.dta" and on deputies' terms from "mandats.dta" that are built in Sub-folder #2.
